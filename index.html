Welcome to the nation of Hydro!

Fontaine is one of the seven regions of Teyvat. It is the nation that worships Lady Furina, the Hydro Archon and God of Justice.

Notable Characters

As the God of Justice, Focalors, she oversees Fontaine as the current Hydro Archon.

Neuvillette is the Iudex of Fontaine, and the leader of the Marechaussee Phantom.
